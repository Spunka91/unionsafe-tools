Frontline Safety Support

This dashboard helps members turn real-world situations into clear safety communication. Type the condition in your own words and use the feedback to help recognize risk, report accurately, and prevent the next injury or event.

1

OSHA Reference Finder

Type any hazard or condition. Example: wet floor, dust, noise, unknown spill, tug issue.

Your OSHA reference guidance will appear here.
2

Is This an OSHA Violation?

Describe what you see. The tool will flag likely safety concerns and possible standards.

Your OSHA concern feedback will appear here.
3

GSAP / Safety Report Wording

Describe the event. This produces fact-based wording without language that can discourage acceptance.

Suggested GSAP/safety report wording will appear here.
4

Injury Reporting Language

Describe the injury or symptoms in your own words. This helps organize factual work-related details.

Suggested factual injury reporting language will appear here.
5

Exposure / PPE Check

Open input for anything encountered: haboob, construction dust, chemical odor, unknown liquid, noise, heat, cold.

Exposure and PPE guidance will appear here.
6

Should I Stop Work?

Type the situation. The tool gives a practical go/no-go style safety response.

Stop-work guidance will appear here.
7

Time Pressure Risk Check

Use this when work is rushed, staffing is thin, or duties are overlapping.

Time-pressure feedback will appear here.
8

Near Miss Builder

Turn “almost happened” events into clear, useful near-miss reports.

Near-miss wording will appear here.
9

Who Do I Report This To?

Describe the issue and get practical routing: supervisor, safety concern, GSAP/ASAP, injury report, environmental, JSC/APC, or emergency response.

Reporting guidance will appear here.
10

Freeze the Scene Tool

Use this after an accident, injury, aircraft damage, equipment contact, spill, or serious near miss.

Freeze-the-scene guidance will appear here.
