Safety Tools Dashboard

Professional field tools for hazard recognition, OSHA reference, GSAP wording, injury reporting, PPE questions, heat risk, and incident response.

Tool 1

OSHA Reference Lookup

Type any condition, hazard, or topic. Examples: wet floor, construction dust, powered truck, noise, chemical odor, heat, blocked exit.

Tool 2

Is This an OSHA Violation?

Describe what you see. The tool will suggest whether it may need escalation and what standards may apply.

Tool 3

GSAP / Safety Report Wording

Use your own words. This tool creates neutral, safety-focused language that avoids blame and avoids operational terms that can distract from safety acceptance.

Tool 4

Injury Reporting Language Assistant

Use this to clearly describe how the injury or illness relates to the work activity, environment, or exposure. This does not guarantee workers’ compensation acceptance.

Tool 5

PPE / Exposure Question Tool

Type any condition: haboob, construction dust, glycol odor, fuel, noise, cold, rain, heat, sharp edges, battery, lavatory service, etc.

Tool 6

Heat Stress Field Check

Enter temperature, humidity, and workload to estimate a heat risk flag.

Tool 7

Freeze the Scene Checklist

Use this after an injury, near miss, aircraft damage, equipment failure, or serious hazard.

Contact

Stay Connected

Share feedback, request updates, or ask to be contacted. Name, email, and organization are not mandatory. When submitted, your email app will open and you must press Send.

⚠️ Safety Disclaimer: This tool is for education and safety support only. It does not replace OSHA regulations, company policy, union agreements, airport rules, medical guidance, legal advice, or workers’ compensation decisions. Always verify through official procedures.
